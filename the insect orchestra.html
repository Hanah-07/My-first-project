The Insect Orchestra

By Hanah Dalvie

Bees in the clover with golden flair,
Buzz like trumpets through scented air.
Ladybugs march in dotted red lines,
Fireflies fliker like neon signs.
Ants build empires beneath the sand,
all working wonders, tiny and grand.

Grasshoppers leap with a fiddler's bend,
Crickets chirp where the shadows blend.
Spiders weave with silky might,
Spinning dreams in the hush of night.
